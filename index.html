hey there!

My name is Maya Koeppen. I am a storyteller, social media guru, and recent University of Maryland graduate with a B.A. in Journalism. I love using visuals to tell stories and am passionate about serving others. Storytelling, for me, is about so much more than the words you write or the photos you take. It is also about the people you impact along the way.

I want my stories to mean something to people, whether it is a source of hope, inspiration, joy, or anything in-between. I want them to have a lasting impact on readers. If one of my stories so much as puts a smile on someone's face, then I'll count that as a success in my books. I hope as you explore my site, that you get a sense of what means the most to me and maybe even find some joy along the way :) Thanks for visiting!
